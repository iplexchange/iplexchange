Privacy Policy

Your Data Security at IPL-Exchange

At IPL-Exchange, we recognize that privacy is a fundamental concern for our users. As India’s most trusted gateway to official cricket IDs, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data protection. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time/date statistics.
  • Communication Data: Records of WhatsApp, Telegram, or Email correspondence for personalized support.
  • Cookies: Small files to enhance experience and remember preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

Strictly used to improve service: Facilitating ID acquisition via WhatsApp, enhancing site security, improving user experience, and sending critical IPL 2026 updates.

3. Data Protection and Encryption

Security is our core philosophy. We use SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) encryption. We do not sell or trade your personal information to outside parties for marketing.

4. Third-Party Links

We contain links to official login portals. These external sites have independent privacy policies. We only partner with exchanges meeting our rigorous standards.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access your data, request deletion, and opt-out of cookies or WhatsApp interactions.

6. Compliance & Changes

We operate in compliance with Indian digital standards and update protocols regularly to align with new legal frameworks. Changes will be posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

For concerns regarding your data, reach out through our official Contact Us channels.
